Privacy Policy — Sikhverse

Last updated: May 11, 2026

Sikhverse ("we", "the app") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

  • Anonymous device identifier — a randomly generated ID stored only on your phone, used to track your prayer streak. Not linked to your name, email, or any personal info.
  • Daily prayer log timestamps — only when you tap "Log Prayer", to compute your streak.

What we DO NOT collect

  • No name, email, phone, or account information
  • No location data
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising IDs
  • No data is shared with any third party

Notifications

If you enable Daily Hukamnama notifications, the app schedules a local notification on your device. No notification token is sent to any server.

External services

The app fetches authentic Gurbani text from BaniDB (a community-verified open API). No personal data is sent to BaniDB.

Data deletion

Uninstalling the app deletes all your local data. There is no remote account to delete.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected in the app's "Last updated" date.
